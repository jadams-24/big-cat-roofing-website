Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you contact us or request a quote, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Property address for service delivery
  • Details about your roofing needs and preferences
  • Insurance information (if applicable for claims)
  • Any other information you choose to provide

Information Automatically Collected

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages you visit on our site
  • Time and date of your visit
  • General geographic location (city/state level)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively for:

  • Providing Services: To prepare quotes, schedule appointments, and perform roofing services
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide updates about your project
  • Customer Service: To address any concerns or warranty issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Business Operations: To improve our services and website functionality

How We Protect Your Information

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Secure storage of customer records
  • Limited access to personal information (only authorized employees)
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Secure transmission of sensitive data

Information Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted contractors or suppliers directly involved in your project (with your consent)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (your information would remain protected)

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request corrections to your information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Ask questions about our privacy practices

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and improve site functionality. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
