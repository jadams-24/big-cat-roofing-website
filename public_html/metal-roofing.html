Metal Roofing Frequently Asked Questions

How much does metal roofing cost in Metro Detroit?

Metal roofing costs $12,000-$35,000 for most homes, depending on material type and home size. Standing seam steel ranges $15,000-$25,000, while premium copper or aluminum costs more. Though higher upfront than asphalt, metal roofs last 3x longer, making them cost-effective long-term.

How long does metal roofing last in Michigan?

Quality metal roofing lasts 50-70 years in Michigan's climate, compared to 15-25 years for asphalt shingles. Metal handles snow loads, ice dams, temperature fluctuations, and severe weather exceptionally well, making it ideal for Michigan conditions.

Is metal roofing noisy during rain or hail?

Modern metal roofing with proper installation and insulation is no noisier than other roofing materials. We install solid sheathing and adequate insulation that dampens sound. Most homeowners report no noticeable noise difference during rain or storms.

What types of metal roofing do you install?

We install standing seam metal roofing, corrugated metal panels, metal shingles, and architectural metal tiles. Materials include galvanized steel, aluminum, copper, and zinc. We offer dozens of colors and finishes to match any architectural style.

Does metal roofing increase home value?

Yes, metal roofing typically increases home value by 1-6% and offers 85-95% return on investment. The durability, energy efficiency, and curb appeal make metal roofing attractive to potential buyers, especially in Michigan's harsh climate.

Can metal roofing be installed over existing shingles?

In some cases, metal roofing can be installed over one layer of existing asphalt shingles, saving removal costs. However, we typically recommend full removal for optimal performance and to inspect the roof deck. We'll assess your specific situation and recommend the best approach.

How energy efficient is metal roofing?

Metal roofing can reduce cooling costs by 10-25% due to its reflective properties. Cool metal roofing systems reflect solar heat, keeping homes cooler in summer. Many metal roofs qualify for energy efficiency tax credits and rebates.

What warranty comes with metal roofing installation?

We provide comprehensive warranties including manufacturer material warranties (20-50 years depending on product) plus our workmanship warranty. Many metal roofing systems come with paint warranties up to 40 years and substrate warranties up to 50 years.

How long does metal roof installation take?

Metal roof installation typically takes 2-5 days depending on home size, complexity, and weather conditions. Standing seam installations may take longer due to precision requirements, while metal panel systems install more quickly. We work efficiently while maintaining quality standards.

Is metal roofing good for snow and ice?

Metal roofing excels in snow and ice conditions. The smooth surface allows snow to slide off naturally, reducing snow load stress. Metal doesn't absorb moisture like other materials, preventing ice dam formation. This makes metal ideal for Michigan's snowy winters.
