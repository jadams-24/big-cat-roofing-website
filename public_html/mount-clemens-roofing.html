Do I need a permit for roof repair in Mount Clemens, MI?

Yes, Mount Clemens requires permits for most roofing work, including full replacements and major repairs. Minor repairs may not require permits. Big Cat Roofing handles all permit applications and ensures compliance with Mount Clemens building codes. Contact the Mount Clemens Building Department at (586) 469-5280 for specific requirements.

Are there special requirements for roofing in Mount Clemens' historic areas?

Yes, Mount Clemens has historic districts with specific preservation requirements. Properties in historic areas may need to maintain architectural character and use appropriate materials.
