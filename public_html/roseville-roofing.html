Do I need a permit for roof repair in Roseville, MI?

How does Roseville's proximity to Lake St. Clair affect my roof?

Roseville's location between Warren and St. Clair Shores means exposure to lake-effect weather patterns including increased humidity, potential for lake-effect snow, and stronger winds. These conditions can affect roofing materials and increase the risk of ice dams. Regular inspections help catch weather-related damage early.
