Residential Roofing FAQs

How long does a roof last in Michigan?

Typical residential roofs in Michigan last 15-20 years due to our harsh weather conditions including heavy snow, ice, and temperature fluctuations.

What are signs I need a new roof?

Look for missing or curled shingles, granules in gutters, water stains on ceilings, daylight through roof boards, or if your roof is approaching 20 years old.

How long does installation take?

Most residential roof replacements are completed in 1-2 days. Professional installation by experienced crews.

Do you offer financing?

We do not currently offer financing. However, our reasonable prices and quality workmanship provide excellent value for your investment.

What's included in your warranty?

Our residential roofing warranty covers workmanship for up to 10 years from installation date, plus manufacturer warranties on materials.

Can you work with my insurance?

Yes, we can assist with insurance claims for storm damage and provide detailed documentation for your claim.
