Types of Storm Damage We Repair

Michigan weather can cause various types of roof damage. Our experienced team handles all storm-related roofing issues.

Hail Damage

Michigan hailstorms can cause significant damage to asphalt shingles, creating dents, cracks, and granule loss that compromises your roof's integrity.

  • Granule loss assessment
  • Shingle impact damage repair
  • Gutter and downspout damage
  • Hidden damage detection

Wind Damage

High winds can lift, tear, or completely remove shingles, exposing your roof deck to water damage and requiring immediate attention.

  • Missing shingle replacement
  • Lifted shingle re-securing
  • Flashing damage repair
  • Structural damage assessment

Fallen Tree Damage

Storm-toppled trees can cause catastrophic roof damage requiring emergency response and comprehensive repair services.

  • Emergency tree removal coordination
  • Structural damage repair
  • Roof deck replacement
  • Complete roof reconstruction

Ice Dam Damage

Michigan's freeze-thaw cycles create ice dams that cause water backup and interior damage requiring specialized repair techniques.

  • Ice dam removal
  • Ventilation improvements
  • Insulation upgrades
  • Water damage restoration

Storm Damage FAQ

Common questions about storm damage roof repair and insurance claims.

Will my insurance cover storm damage repairs?

Most homeowner's insurance policies cover storm damage including hail, wind, and fallen tree damage. We work directly with your insurance company to ensure maximum coverage and handle all documentation.

How quickly can you respond to emergency calls?

We provide rapid emergency response to storm damage calls throughout Metro Detroit. Our priority is securing your property with emergency tarping and immediate repairs to prevent further damage.

Do you provide emergency tarping services?

Yes, emergency tarping is often our first response to storm damage. This protects your home from further water damage while we arrange for permanent repairs and work with your insurance company.

What should I do immediately after storm damage?

Ensure your family's safety first, then call us at 248-709-3746. Don't attempt roof repairs yourself. Document visible damage with photos and contact your insurance company to start a claim.

How do you work with insurance adjusters?

We coordinate directly with insurance adjusters, providing detailed damage assessments, repair estimates, and documentation. Our experience helps ensure nothing is missed and you receive fair compensation.

Can you help with interior water damage?

While we specialize in roofing, we coordinate with trusted restoration partners for interior damage. Our priority is stopping the source of water intrusion and protecting your property.
