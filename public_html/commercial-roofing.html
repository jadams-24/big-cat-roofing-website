Commercial Roofing FAQs

What is a No Dollar Limit (NDL) warranty?

An NDL warranty provides complete coverage with no cap on repair costs. Our MuleHide certification allows us to offer up to 30-year NDL warranties on qualifying commercial projects.

How long does commercial roof installation take?

Project timeline depends on size and complexity. Professional installation by experienced crews. We work efficiently to minimize business disruption.

Do you offer maintenance programs?

Yes, we offer bi-annual inspection and maintenance programs. Regular maintenance extends roof life and helps prevent emergency repairs.

What types of flat roofs do you install?

We install all major commercial roofing systems including TPO, EPDM, PVC, modified bitumen, and built-up roofing (BUR) systems.

Can you work around our business hours?

Absolutely. We understand the importance of maintaining operations and can schedule work during off-hours or weekends as needed.

What size projects do you handle?

We typically handle commercial projects ranging from $50,000 to $250,000, though we evaluate all projects individually.
